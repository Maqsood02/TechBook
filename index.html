About TechBook

TechBook is a premium collegiate utility platform developed to simplify daily academic administration. With TechBook, students can easily track and record lecture attendance, access digital notes, study packages, and syllabus updates, and challenge themselves with interactive, self-paced quizzes. Course instructors and administrators benefit from automated session controls, report exports, and immediate database syncing.

Built using modern, robust web standards including Firebase Auth and Firestore, TechBook ensures that your academic records are kept safe, secure, and always accessible when you need them.
